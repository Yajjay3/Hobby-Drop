Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Overview

Hobby Drop ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the Hobby Drop browser extension and website.

Information We Collect

Hobby Drop does not collect, store, or transmit personal information on its own for the core free experience. Specifically:

  • We do not track your browsing activity.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or location just to use the free extension.
  • We do not use analytics or advertising trackers inside the extension.

Local Storage

The extension uses browser storage to save your selected hobby, category preferences, cached daily fact data, premium state, and emoji customization so the extension works properly from day to day. This data is stored locally or in your browser sync storage and is not sold to third parties.

Payments and Premium Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed through ExtensionPay and Stripe. If you subscribe, payment information such as your email address and billing details is handled directly by ExtensionPay and Stripe. Hobby Drop does not receive or store your full payment card details.

Public Fact Sources

Hobby Drop may request public information from sources such as Wikipedia and Wikimedia in order to display fact content, “on this day” style information, and learn-more links related to the hobbies you select.

Website Suggestions

If you voluntarily submit a hobby or category suggestion through the website form, that suggestion is sent by email so it can be reviewed for possible future additions to Hobby Drop.

Children's Privacy

Hobby Drop is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or need support, please visit wizardanddroid.com/hobbydrop/.
